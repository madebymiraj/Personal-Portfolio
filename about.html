Me. In a nutshell.

I am 21 years old and I have been professionally designing for about six years, both independently and as part of a team. I have been an intern every year since I graduated from high school in 2011. I have done internships for Ande & Partners, iSpeech, Vydia, and Hertz.

I see my previous experiences as a journey that I have been taking with design; using what I learn from my past experience to bring to my next.

 

I'm a pretty cool kid.

I attend New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT) pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a concentration in Web Applications and a minor in Human-Computer Interaction. I expect to graduate in May 2016.
